About Us

Sea of Life Farm Produce is a Ugandan-based agribusiness established in 2024, committed to supplying fresh, natural, and high-quality farm products.

We specialize in carefully selected farm produce and livestock products, with a strong focus on cleanliness, proper handling, and consistency in supply. From well-sorted Masavu beans and freshly pounded groundnuts to pure dairy milk and cleaned whole broiler chicken, we ensure that every product meets our quality standards.

In addition to the already mentioned products, we also deal in live animals including cows, bulls, goats, and heifers, supplied on request.

At Sea of Life Farm Produce, we believe in honesty, reliability, and building long-term relationships with our customers. Whether you are buying for your home or your business, we are dedicated to serving you with integrity and care.
